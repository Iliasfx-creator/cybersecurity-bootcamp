# Safety Scope

## Allowed targets

- http://127.0.0.1:8004
- http://localhost:8004

## Disallowed targets

No public websites, university systems, company systems, accounts, or third-party applications.

## Lab rule

DOM XSS payloads are used only against my own local intentionally vulnerable page.

## Why this matters

Testing XSS on systems without permission is not acceptable.

Even simple payloads can trigger logs, alerts, disruption, or legal problems.

This lab is only for controlled local learning.
