# Safety Scope

## Allowed targets

- http://127.0.0.1:8002
- http://localhost:8002

## Disallowed targets

No public websites, university systems, company systems, accounts, or third-party applications.

## Lab rule

The XSS payloads in this lab are used only against my own local intentionally vulnerable app.

I will not test XSS payloads on systems I do not own or do not have explicit permission to test.

I will not paste payloads into public websites, university systems, company systems, real accounts, or third-party applications.

## Why this matters

Testing XSS on systems without permission is not acceptable.

Even harmless-looking payloads can create alerts, logs, disruption, or legal issues.

A payload that is safe in a local lab can be treated as hostile behavior on a real system.

The purpose of this lab is to understand the root cause and fix in a controlled environment.
