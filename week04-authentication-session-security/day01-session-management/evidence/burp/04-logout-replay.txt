Comparison 4: Logout revokes the session and replay fails
Capture status: COMPLETED

OBSERVED LOGOUT RESPONSE
HTTP/1.1 200 OK
Set-Cookie: session=; Max-Age=0; Path=/; HttpOnly; SameSite=Lax

REPLAY REQUEST
GET /me HTTP/1.1
Host: 127.0.0.1:8013
Cookie: session=REDACTED_OLD

OBSERVED REPLAY RESPONSE
HTTP/1.1 401 Unauthorized

Security conclusion:
Cookie deletion is paired with server-side revocation, so a copied old cookie
cannot be replayed after logout.
